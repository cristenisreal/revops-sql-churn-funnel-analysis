# Territory Prioritization Score (Country-wide Sales Strategy)

## Objective
Rank territories by revenue upside to guide where Ops/Revenue should focus next.

## Key Signals
- Demand: shift volume
- Supply friction: unfilled shifts / fill rate
- Revenue: total revenue and revenue per shift
- Upside score: revenue-weighted gap from perfect fill
